York Dragon Boat Race

ENG1 Team 11 project

About

Our project is centered around developing a dragon boat racing game. It is designed to mimic a real dragon boat race, however we have decided upon certain differences, both to make the programming of the game more feasible, and to make the game more engaging to the user. One such difference is the ability to buy boat upgrades between races.
